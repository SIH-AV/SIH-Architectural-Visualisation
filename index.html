Over 15 years of 3D visualisation production, SIH Architectural Visualisation is a CGI studio based in Melbourne. We produce CGI and Photomontage images that focus on architectural, Interior design, and urban planning 3D visuals for town planning, marketing, and competition purposes.

Our visual collection is inspired by conceptual art and realism where we bring the designer's idea/concept to life for a better understanding of the architectural spaces and scale of the buildings. We have very competitive prices than the market for quality work.

With architecture and digital design background, we believe that our team has the expertise and skills to fulfill all types of 3D visualization requirements for architects, Interior designers, urban planners, and developers. We've come together to do what we are passionate about, to improve and grow from one another's achievements.
