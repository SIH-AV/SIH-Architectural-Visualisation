Over 15 years of 3D visualisation production, SIH Architectural Visualisation is a CGI studio based in Melbourne. We produce CGI images that focus on architectural, Interior design, and urban planning 3D visuals for town planning, marketing, and competition purposes.

Our visual collection is inspired by conceptual art and realism where we bring the designer's idea/concept to life for a better understanding of the architectural spaces and scale. We have very competitive prices than the market for quality work.

With architecture and digital design background, we believe that our team has the expertise and skills to fulfill all types of 3D visualization requirements for architects, Interior designers, urban planners, and developers. We’ve come together to do what we are passionate about, to improve and grow from one another’s achievements.

Services

A well-executed visualization is a powerful tool. At SIH AV, we meticulously evaluate each design to find the ideal composition, the most flattering angles, and inspired styling. We produce exceptional architectural renderings for clients in Perth, Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and across the globe.

Marketing Visuals Strategy:

Being able to sell unbuilt property off-plan is a crucial element to the success of a project. A series of high quality 3D visuals will:

• Elevate the perceived value of your product

• Spark desire and drive demand for your project

• Accelerate sales

• Differentiate your project from competitors

• Enhance your brand value

Investing in high-quality, photo-realistic architectural visualization yields substantial returns. It adds value to your project, creates desire for your design, and helps you achieve faster sales at a premium price. Your project will stand out, generate buzz, and actively engage potential buyers and clients.

We also offer the services of an experienced architectural photographer for street view and aerial drone photography. These images can be used in town planning applications and as photomontage backdrops for council submissions.
